Newsflash

With Storybook Memoria keeping the overview over your story becomes even easier. Choose a character, select a date and see everything that happens at the specified date in a clearly represented graph. With Memoria you can "travel" through your entire story in a completely new way. Think of it as of a dynamic, interactive mind map. Check out the new Memoria Tool.
